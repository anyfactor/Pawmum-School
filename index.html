Pawmum School

Pawmum Thrakla

Pawmum Tharkla is a residential school of Mro community, located in Lama, Bandarban, Bangladesh.

Pawmum Tharkla is a residential school catering to provide free and quality education to the children of the indigenous tribe of Mro, in the secluded hillside of Lama, Bandarban, Bangladesh.

The seclusion of the Mro tribe within the deep hillside jungle has no access to paved roads, and the journey from civilization is often enduring.

The tribe has a whole has limited access to public welfare and support. A group of young people decided to take them upon themselves to ensure basic education to the young generation so that they can bring about prosperity to their lives and their small community. So, started Pawmum Tharkla. The people who run this school not only provide education, and guidance to go forward in life, they are also continuous provide basic amenities such as medical support.

But unfortunately being located in the most impoverished and isolated part of the world, the concern for the welfare of the Mro people is often disregarded. Our school needs very little to stay operational, but we are often finding it difficult to manage the little funding we need.
